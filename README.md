This is my repository for my MiniPod Ultra Project. This project aims to re-imagine how one uses an Apple Watch and rework that entirely.
Spanning from the form factor and its physical footprint upto refining it with custom software to achieve an integrated experience such that similar to an iPod or an MP3 player.

The inspiration and reasoning / rational for all of this came from when I found myself struggling a lot with ADHD and digital distractions where I found myselfbeing unable to do even the simplest of things
on my phone without getting distracted.

Solutions like buying something like a dumb phone or whatever doesn't work well in an era of technology, 
especially me as in the field of computer science - I still need to be connected but in a in a more controlled fashion.

And hence where this comes. Unlike a dumb phone or MP3 players (most), here you can store data on your watch and even listen to music via bluetooth earphones. Yet still on track with emails and phones.
There's so much more potential when looking into potential users: say pagers in a hospital with updated tech, anyone who wants to decentralize their smartphone and focus,
those who don't want much interaction with technology but need to share their location and contact emergency contacts and even people who simply want to just take casul walks on the beach or wherever
and not stress about theft (most would rather a watch stolen than a phone).
With the ability to use a calculator, calendar and setting up alarms - this really makes this idea quite promising.
